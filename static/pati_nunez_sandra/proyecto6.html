Proyectos

INNOVAL

2006

Alimentaria has always based its identity on the image of an egg and Innoval uses the same concept, though for this exhibition of new food products we have always worked with more abstract versions. In this case, we used photographs of egg-shaped forms made from coloured cellophane, a way to express space (the exhibition) and movement (innovation).
