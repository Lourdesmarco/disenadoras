2017. MADRID

Ferpal Sans is a typography designed for Madrid. From the lack of identity and homogeneity in the typographies of the city, I imagined how a typeface would unify this problem. Designed especially for signage, taking as reference the ceramic signs of the streets of Madrid and the patterns of design of typography for large applications, it is perfectly suited for graphic design applications ranging from editorial, corporate, web, interaction to product design.
