About me:

My name is Silvia and I'm a designer & artist based in Madrid. In 2019, I was honored with the Spanish National Prize of Design.

Being a freelancer allows me to collaborate with design consultancies, studios, and clients from around the world. I’m normally involved in projects as a digital, brand, or graphic designer. It also allows me to follow my creative side by producing custom pieces and participating in exhibitions at galleries and museums.

In the past, I have been part of Ogilvy&Mather, Designit Madrid, and Designit New York. I was also fortunate enough to live in Italy thanks to the Spanish Academy of Rome.
